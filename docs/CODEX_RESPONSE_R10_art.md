# R10 Art Overhaul Response

## Art Bible Summary

- Palette: deep void blue-black base with rift cyan and violet energy, ember-orange for danger/shop/evolution accents.
- Glow rules: rift seams, player squad, projectiles, loot, elites, bosses, hazards, and kill bursts glow. Normal enemies get restrained glow only.
- Readability order: player and incoming danger > enemy threat tier > projectiles/hit feedback > pickups > background.
- Shape rules: enemies use size, glow radius, and marker shape in addition to color; elites and bosses are not color-only.
- UI: 8 px panel/card radius, cyan border, dark translucent fill, violet focus/pressed states, amber rare/evolution emphasis, compact icon-led HUD.

See `docs/ART_DIRECTION_R10.md` for the full direction.

## Internal Review

### Round 1

Art Team proposal:
- Use dense blue-violet void background, cyan/violet rift seams, additive glow sprites for compatibility renderer.
- Add glow to every projectile and pickup so the old "colored dot" read disappears.
- Add large elite/Boss aura and stronger death particles to sell impact.

Player Experience challenge:
- 150 enemies plus projectile trails can become a cyan smear.
- Pickup glow could compete with normal enemy silhouettes.
- Elite color alone fails color-blind readability.
- Background cracks must not look like enemy telegraphs.

Art Team revision:
- Projectile trails are short, alpha-capped, and bound to projectile pools; script cap is `160`.
- Pickup glow alpha is lower than enemy glow and far below elite/Boss markers.
- Elite/Boss readability uses size, ring radius, and marker geometry.
- Background crack tiling is low alpha; only the central rift has stronger additive light, still behind gameplay.

### Round 2

Art Team proposal:
- Global UI Theme turns all modal cards into bordered rift panels.
- HUD gains icon-backed strips and XP trough/fill.
- Death bursts add CPUParticles2D for combat reward.

Player Experience challenge:
- UI hover/focus cannot reduce card text contrast.
- Hit-stop must not pause modal flows or leave time scale altered.
- Particles must remain pooled and capped.
- Screen shake must honor the existing settings toggle.

Final decision:
- Theme uses high-contrast off-white text, cyan hover border, violet pressed fill, and amber only for special emphasis.
- Hit-stop lives in `GameManager.request_combat_impact()` and resets `Engine.time_scale`; it is only called on elite/Boss death.
- Death particles live inside the existing `death_burst` pool cap (`20` live cap from `EntityFactory`).
- Screen shake is routed through `Hero.request_screen_shake()` and checks `PlayerSettings.screen_shake_enabled`.
